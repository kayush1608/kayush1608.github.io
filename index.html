My Profile

My home in village Vallabhipur

Ancient Darbargarh located in my village

The drone view of my village

I was born in a village named Vallabhipur, located in Bhavnagar, Gujarat. It's a peaceful and beautiful place, known for its natural charm and cultural richness. The village has a calm and welcoming vibe, with fields, open skies, and a strong sense of community. One of the most notable landmarks in Vallabhipur is the historic Darbargadh, an old royal palace that reflects the heritage and architectural beauty of the region. The village is also home to several temples, adding to its spiritual atmosphere. Growing up there has given me a deep appreciation for simplicity, tradition, and the beauty of rural life.


Historically, the region holds significance as the former capital of the Maitraka Dynasty, which ruled from 470 AD to 788 AD
